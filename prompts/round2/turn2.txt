Output the classification vector for every glyph. Strict format, one line per glyph:

<glyph> | C:<CAT> | A:<ARITY> | D:<DIR> | F:<FAIL>

Where:
  CAT   = SUB | PRO | REL | MOD | STR
  ARITY = 0 | 1 | 2 | N
  DIR   = L | R | S | O
  FAIL  = SAT | STALL | BOOM | NULL

SORTING: Group by Category (SUB → PRO → REL → MOD → STR).

EVERY input glyph must appear EXACTLY once. No extras. No omissions. No prose. No headers.

Examples:
物 | C:SUB | A:0 | D:O | F:SAT
化 | C:PRO | A:2 | D:R | F:STALL
⇒ | C:REL | A:2 | D:S | F:BOOM
大 | C:MOD | A:1 | D:R | F:NULL
中 | C:STR | A:2 | D:S | F:SAT
